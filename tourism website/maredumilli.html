Maredumilli is a village in Maredumilli Mandal in Alluri Sitharama Raju district in the state of Andhra Pradesh in India. It is 90 km from Rajahmundry city. It has many waterfalls and beautiful locations. Over the years, it has become a popular location for various films and television shows.

-Maredumilli is one of the best tourist spots of Eastern Ghats, which is 111 km away from Vijayawada city. The Anathagiri and sukarimetta forest are part of maredumilli Valley. -Bongulo chicken is one which is famous here. All Non Vegetarians want to have once in a life this bongulo chicken. Don't miss it in your next trip.
