Dwaraka Tirumala temple, located in Andhra Pradesh, is a significant pilgrimage site for Hindus. Also known as “Chinna Tirupati” (Little Tirupati), it holds religious importance due to the presence of the renowned Sri Venkateswara Swamy temple. This temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. The architecture of the temple is mesmerizing, showcasing intricate carvings and sculptures that reflect the rich cultural heritage of the region.
